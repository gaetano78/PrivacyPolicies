Privacy Policy for Face Symmetry - NO ADS

Face Symmetry - No ADS is made available only on iOS through the Apple App Store, and is designed variously for use on iPad, iPhone, and/or iPod touch. The specific device(s) supported are noted in the individual app descriptions found on this website. The apps fall under Apple’s Standard License Agreement found here:

http://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/appstore/dev/stdeula/ 

Apple shares information with the developer each day regarding the number of Face Symmetry - No ADS app downloads, the devices and the countries on which the app is downloaded, price paid, whether or not the app was downloaded by an educational institution, and the number of views on the App Store. Apple also shares with the developer the aggregated “opt-in” information that users agree to regarding diagnostics and usage of the apps.

Apple does not share any information with the developer regarding the names of individuals or institutions that download the app. The only way the developer will know if a specific user download and use the app is if the user contact the developer through email. 

The app does not collect any user information, nor contains advertising or in-app purchases.
